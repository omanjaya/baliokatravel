About This Experience

Immerse yourself in the spiritual heart of Bali with our Sacred Temple Tour. This journey takes you to the island's most revered temples, where ancient traditions and stunning architecture create an unforgettable experience.

Your expert guide will share the rich history and cultural significance of each temple, explaining the intricate carvings, symbolic offerings, and daily rituals that have been preserved for centuries. You'll witness firsthand the deep spiritual connection that Balinese people have with their sacred spaces.

This tour includes visits to three major temples: Tirta Empul with its holy spring waters, Tanah Lot perched on a rocky outcrop in the sea, and Ulun Danu Beratan nestled by the lake. Each offers a unique glimpse into Bali's diverse religious practices and breathtaking natural beauty.

What should I wear for the temple visit?

Both men and women should cover their shoulders and knees. Sarongs are usually available for rent at temple entrances if needed.

Can I participate in the water purification ritual?

Yes, visitors are welcome to participate in the purification ritual at Tirta Empul. Your guide will explain the proper procedure and etiquette.

Is this tour suitable for children?

Yes, this tour is family-friendly. However, there is some walking involved, so it may not be suitable for very young children or those with mobility issues.

What happens if it rains?

The tour operates in light rain. Umbrellas are provided. In case of severe weather, the itinerary may be adjusted for your safety and comfort.
